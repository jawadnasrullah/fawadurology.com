Dr. Fawad Nasrullah is a consultant urologist, and a specialist of male sexual health, infertility, erectile dysfunction, andrology and advanced endoscopic urology. He has twenty years of experience in medicine and surgery including fifteen years of solid post-specialization experience.

Dr. Fawad Nasrullah operates at several hospitals in Lahore area, offering patients a variety of choice for their convenience. All of these hospitals are fully equipped and staffed for routine surgical and urological procedures.

Dr. Fawad Nasrullah primarily focuses on male infertility, erectile dysfunction, penile implants, Andrology, and advanced endoscopic procedures but he also performs all urological surgeries as well which include:

  • General Urological Procedures / Pediatric Urology

  • Microsurgical varicocelectomy

  • Microsurgical vasovasostomies and vasoepididmostomies

  • Vasectomy reversal

  • Penile implants (Coloplast and AMS, malleable and inflatable prostheses, three piece)

  • Testicular implants

  • Surgery for penile venous leak

  • Routine and laser TURP

  • PCNL with state of the art EMS pneumatic and Richard Wolf ultrasonic lithoclast

  • Ureterorenoscopies (URS)

  • Hypospadias and epispadias reconstruction
